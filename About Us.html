Age comes with many difficulties that we know. So we come in to help.

Rainbow Eldery Support[RES UGANDA]is an initiative incorporated in 2022. At the apex of its governance is the board of Directors, which is responsible for making polices and determine strategic program areas.The board appoints full and part-time professionals as well as volunteers who undertake delivery of program activities.
RES UGANDA is a fully professional entity with its own premises for offices situated at plot 4 Spring road Bugoloobi, Kampala.
